Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Master Aviator: Event Horizon ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. The App is developed and maintained by Mateusz Wiśniewski ("Developer").

1. No Personal Data Collection

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from users. We do not request or store names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other sensitive data.

2. No Internet Connectivity

The App works completely offline. It does not connect to the internet, and therefore, no data is transmitted to any server.

3. No Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, APIs, ad networks, analytics tools, or social media integrations.

4. Local Storage Only

All game progress and settings are stored locally on the user's device. This data is not transmitted or shared with any external systems or services.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years of age. Since no data is collected, the App complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

6. Data Security

Although no personal data is collected or transmitted, we still ensure that the application is designed to be secure and does not introduce any vulnerabilities to the user’s device.

7. User Rights

Since the App does not collect or store any user data, there is no information to access, correct, or delete. However, users may remove the App from their device at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Effective date” at the top of this page. We encourage users to review this policy periodically.
